What's up with the Squirrel ?

Once upon a time, there was a hacker camp. Internet was working fine until a rodent ate a fiber cable. Said rodent became the most hated creature in a 10km radius. The NOC called for an illustration about a "cute" rodent. Turns out, Anita Niskala (a member of our hackerspace) thought that she was not going to make it cute. She was going to make it an evil squirrel.

Up to this day, this squirrel is printed on the DWDM boxes used at the camp. It's also a known easter egg for who knows where to look.
